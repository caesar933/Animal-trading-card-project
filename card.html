Boxer Dog

First German Police Dog

According to the American Kennel Club, The Boxer was one of the first breeds used in Germany for police training.

  • Personality: Fun-loving, bright, active and loyal
  • Height: 23-25 inches (male), 21.5-23.5 inches (female)
  • Weight: 65-80 pounds (male), females are about 15 pounds less than male
  • Life Expectancy: 10-12 years

A well-conditioned Boxer is an awesome sight. Their muscles ripple beneath their short, tight-fitting coat. The dark brown eyes and wrinkled forehead give the face an alert, curious look.
